Meet the Founders

The Manga Boys began as a dream shared by two passionate creators — one who brings characters to life through art and another who builds digital worlds where stories live.

Ajin
Illustration

Ajin is the creative soul behind the visuals. From character sketches to full manga panels, his artwork gives The Manga Boys its unique identity.

Abel
Web Designing

Abel transforms ideas into interactive experiences. Every pixel, page, and layout you see is carefully crafted to bring manga closer to fans.

How It All Started

What began as a hobby — sharing, reading, and discussing manga — soon evolved into a platform. With Ajin's talent for illustration and Abel's passion for digital creation, The Manga Boys was born. Today, they provide curated manga, ebooks, and collectible prints to fans who love unique stories and characters.
